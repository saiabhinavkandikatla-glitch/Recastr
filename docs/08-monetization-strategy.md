# Monetization strategy

## Positioning

Recastr sells speed and content leverage:

"Turn one video, podcast, or blog into 30 platform-ready content assets."

## Plans

### Free

Price: INR 0

Limits:
- 3 projects per month
- YouTube metadata, blog, and text ingestion
- X and LinkedIn outputs
- Copy only
- Watermarked preview

Goal:
- Activation and virality.

Upgrade triggers:
- 3 project limit reached
- Export clicked
- Instagram/Reel generation clicked
- Schedule clicked

### Starter

Price: INR 499/month

Limits:
- 15 projects per month
- X, LinkedIn, Instagram captions
- PDF/CSV export
- Basic tone rewrite

Goal:
- Solo creator entry plan.

### Pro

Price: INR 999/month

Limits:
- Unlimited reasonable projects with fair use
- Full platform pack
- Viral Hook Intelligence
- Reel scripts
- YouTube Community posts
- Brand voice profiles
- Scheduling beta

Goal:
- Main paid plan.

### Team

Price: INR 2999/month

Limits:
- Team workspace
- 5 seats
- Client folders
- Shared brand voices
- Advanced exports
- Priority processing

Goal:
- Agencies and creator teams.

## Revenue model

Primary:
- Monthly subscriptions.

Secondary:
- Annual plan with 2 months free.
- Usage overage for transcription-heavy accounts.
- Agency add-on seats.

## Pricing page copy

Free:
"Try the workflow and generate your first content packs."

Starter:
"For creators who publish weekly and want faster repurposing."

Pro:
"For serious creators turning every source into a full content engine."

Team:
"For agencies and teams managing multiple brands."

## Metrics

Activation:
- First source pasted
- First hook generated
- First copy action
- First export

Conversion:
- Free limit hit
- Upgrade modal viewed
- Checkout started
- Payment captured

Retention:
- Projects created per week
- Copy/export actions per project
- Hook generation usage
- Brand voice usage
